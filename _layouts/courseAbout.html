Organizers

  • Robin Lovelace is the course leader as part of his role as Research Fellow in the Consumer Data Research Centre at the University of Leeds. Robin learned spatial microsimulation and R during his PhD on the energy costs of commuting and has developed new methods in spatial microsimulation and applied them to real-world transport problems.
  • Phil Mike Jones was conscripted to demonstrate on the course and has ended up helping out with its planning. A PhD student at the University of Sheffield, he is implementing spatial microsimulation methods for health care applications. Phil is also an experienced user of R for statistic analysis and processing.
